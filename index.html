◈    Medical Costs in the United States    ◈

This visualization is designed to help understand the relationship between an individual’s characteristics like sex, BMI, and age – with their yearly medical expenses

The data used in this visualization is acquired through Kaggle and was taken in 2018

There are a total of 1338 datapoints in this dataset, and we will be using histograms and scatterplots to visualize the relationships between different variables
